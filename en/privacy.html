Boom Candy Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Boom Candy ("we," "our," or "this app"). We take your privacy seriously, and this privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Information We Collect

Boom Candy is a local game application. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information.

This app runs locally on your device. All game data (including game records, player settings, etc.) is stored only on your device locally and is not uploaded to any server.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

This app may collect the following anonymous technical information (only for improving app performance):

  • App crash logs (for fixing issues)
  • Device type and operating system version (for compatibility optimization)

This information is anonymous and cannot identify you.

3. Information Use

We use the collected information only for:

  • Improving app performance and stability
  • Fixing technical issues
  • Optimizing user experience

4. Information Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not store any of your data on servers.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not include any third-party analytics services, advertising services, or data collection services.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security is entirely controlled by your device's security settings. We recommend that you:

  • Regularly back up your device
  • Use device passwords or biometric authentication to protect your device

7. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, you do not need to worry about data breaches or misuse of personal information.

9. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be notified to you through app updates. Continued use of this app indicates your acceptance of the updated privacy policy.
